Hello, my name is Jacob Willis. I am a Software Engineer who has studied at the University of Technology of Sydney (UTS). I am and have always been intrested in technology, particularly coding and developing. One of the main parts I enjoy about it is developing new and interesting pieces of software and being able to see it work and used by others.

At UTS I completed my honours project. As a part of that I worked with C++ and Unreal Engine to create a graphics simulation. This simulation was designed to help understand how cells fuse together under different environments. This was going to be used to help better understand 3D printing organic material. Specifically heart matter. Since then I have worked in various software roles both as a full stack engineer and an applications developer. I am continuously trying to improve my skills and work on different projects.
